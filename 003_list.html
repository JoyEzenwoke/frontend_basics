How To Prepare My Favourite Soup


Okro Soup


Ingredients

  • Stockfish - Pre soaked .
  • Dry Fish - cleaned and soaked .
  • Ponmo(cow skin) - cooked .
  • Crayfish - 2 tablespoons, blended .
  • Pepper - scotch bonnet or chili (blended with onions).
  • Seasoning cubes - like knorr or maggi .
  • Salt - to taste.
  • Ugwu(fluted pumpkin leaves) or spinach- a handful, chopped .
  • Onions - 1 small (optional for extra flavour.
  • Protein water .

Steps

  1. In a pot, heat palm oil for about 30seconds (do not bleach it ).
  2. Add blended pepper and a little onion, fry for about 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add the protein and stir well .
  4. Add crayfish ,more seasonning cubes if needed and a salt to taste .
  5. Stir in chopped okro.You will noticed the soup becomes stretchy/slimy.
  6. Finally, toss in the chopped ugwu or spinach leaves .
  7. Stir and let everything cook together for another 2-3minutes .
  8. Check for salt and seasonning .

Summary

 It is easy to prepare with the right ingredients.
